A little bit about me.

I currently reside in the Orlando, Florida area, where I spend my days creating websites, training in Olympic weightlifting, and obsessing over podcasts. My journey into tech started after graduating from Stanford University while working in Operations at a Silicon Valley startup. As I began editing and writing for the company’s customer knowledge base, I fell in love with creating clear and helpful content. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to expand upon my copywriting skills and create my own blog and website.

That curiosity opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom, challenge, and versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I’m diving into front-end development, where I can use my organizational skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.  And, I continue to express my love of writing as a freelance blogger, copywriter, and creative writer.

And stay tuned for my up and coming, revamped website!  Launching by October 21st.
